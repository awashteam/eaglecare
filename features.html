About Us

Eagle Care Transportation, Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT), services provide reliable, safe, and timely transportation for individuals who need assistance getting to and from medical appointments, patients with chronic conditions, seniors, and individuals with disabilities who may face barriers accessing healthcare due to transportation challenges. Our NEMT solutions ensure clients reach their destinations comfortably, helping improve health outcomes and reduce missed appointments.
